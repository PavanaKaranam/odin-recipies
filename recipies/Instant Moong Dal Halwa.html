Moong Dal Halwa

Dish Description:

Moong Dal Ka halwa is a winter favourite recipe. Cooking moong dal halwa can be time consuming & complicated so my home made recipe for halwa is simple easy and best!

Ingredients:

  • Moong dal, no skin (मूंग दाल) – ½ cup
  • Ghee(घी)- 3 tbsp
  • Sooji (सूजी) – 1 tbsp
  • Besan (बेसन) – 1 tbsp
  • Nuts(ड्राई फ्रुट) chopped – handfu

    • For Syrup:

  • Water (पानी) – 1 cup
  • Sugar (चीनी) – ½ cup
  • Cardamom powder (इलायची पाउडर) – ½ tsp
  • Milk (दूध) – ½ cup

Steps:

  1. Roast the moong dal on low flame, till it gets a darker shade of brown. Be sure to stir it constantly or else it won’t get brown evenly.
  2. At the same time in a pan, add water and sugar to make a thin sugar syrup. Add cardamom powder to it and add milk when the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Now, cool down and grind the moong dal in two batches so that it gets ground evenly.
  4. In a pan, heat some ghee and then add the ground moong dal, sooji, and besan, then roast it thoroughly.
  5. Now, add the sugar syrup into it and stir it well for a few minutes until it thickens. Then add the chopped nuts.
  6. It’s done! The simplest way to prepare moong dal ka halwa is here. Enjoy it hot!

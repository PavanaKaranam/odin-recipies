Shakshuka

Dish Description:

Shakshuka means mixture. It is a poached egg dish from the Maghreb region and has many versions of it from each country or community that makes it. Let's look at one of suck recipes!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil– 3 tbsp
  • Garlic, chopped– 2 tsp
  • Ginger, chopped– ½ inch pc
  • Onion, diced– 1 cu
  • Capscium, diced– 1 cup
  • Tomato, diced– 1 cup
  • Green chilli, chopped– 1 no
  • Fresh Tomato puree– 2 cups
  • Kashmiri chilli powder– 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder– ½ tsp
  • Salt– to taste
  • Sugar– ½ tsp
  • Cheese grated– ½ cup
  • Eggs– 4-5 nos
  • Black olives– handful
  • Green olives– handful
  • Coriander, chopped– handful

Accompaniments

  • Sourdough bread slices

Steps

  1. Heat a pan and drizzle olive oil.
  2. Once hot add garlic, ginger and give a quick stir, do not brown it.
  3. Add onions, tomato, capsicum and green chillies together and on high heat toss them for a minute.
  4. Sprinkle chilli powder, turmeric and stir and then pour the fresh tomato puree. Sprinkle salt and a pinch of sugar and cook on high heat for 3-4 mins.
  5. Now lower the heat and separately break an egg in a bowl.
  6. Using the back of the spoon make a little space or depressing in the centre of the pan and pour the egg in that cavity.
  7. Similarly break the remaining eggs and pour it in the pan. Sprinkle some grated cheese over the the entire dish.
  8. Cover the pan with a lid and let it cook on low heat for 4-6 mins or till the egg yolk is cooked but still runny.
  9. Remove the lid and garnish it with olives and chopped coriander and serve it hot with crisp bread .

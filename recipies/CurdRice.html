Curd Rice

Dish Description:

Curd Rice is a simple and uncomplicated recipe from South India, which is simply curd, rice and tempering. It is one of the homiest and comforting of meals. let us look at the ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Rice, boiled- 2 cups
  • Curd- 1½ cups
  • Ginger, chopped- 1½ tbsp
  • Green Chill, chopped- – 2 nos
  • Dry red chillies– 2-3 nos
  • Mustard seeds– 2 tsp
  • Urad dal- 2 tspv
  • Asafoetida– ¼ tsp
  • Curry leaves– handful
  • Salt– to taste
  • Coriander, chopped– handful

Steps

  1. For tempering, in a hot pan, add some oil, dried red chillies, mustard seeds, urad dal
  2. take some boiled rice in a flat bowl, and add some curd. Chop ginger, chop some green chillies, and add them to the rice and curd
  3. Finally, adjust salt and add soe pickle to the side and enjoy!
